    Research

    My Research Scope

    My research focuses on interregional trade networks in the Central and Northern Andes. I have previously worked on archaeological projects in Trujillo, Cañete, Ancash, Castellammare di Stabia (Italy), and the Midwestern United States. My interests are focused on the integration of GIS, network theory, and environmental archaeology. I have presented research related to Zooarchaeology, Archaeobotany and GIS.

    I can be found on Researchgate and Academia.edu
